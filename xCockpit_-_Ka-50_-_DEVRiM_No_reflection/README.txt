This mod provides slightly dirty windows and removes all baked reflections on some glass materials in the Ka-50 cockpit such as all windows, Shkval screen, ABRIS screen and two tracking sensors glasses. The reflections on HUD glasses were dimmed a little bit for realism.

Retrait des reflets et salissure légère des vitres dans le Ka-50.

---------------------------------------
-- Tested with DCS World 2.5.6.45915 --
---------------------------------------
